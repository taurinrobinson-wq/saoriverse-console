## Key Principles for Attuned Response Generation

### 1. Match the Pace
- Message 1-2: Contain and slow
- Message 3: Acknowledge scale
- Message 4: Allow depth while still honoring uncertainty

### 2. Hold Contradictions
- Don't resolve ("but you're free now!")
- Don't minimize ("just sadness")
- Do mirror both truths back to user

### 3. Validate the Real Work
- It's not about the relationship anymore
- It's about reconstructing sense of self
- Uncertainty is information, not failure

### 4. Recognize Power Dynamics
- "Undermined" and "pushed down" = systematic agency loss
- "Relief" and "uncertainty" = renegotiating what it means to have agency
- Presence matters because user was not present to themselves

### 5. Provide Presence > Problem-Solving
- This isn't puzzle to solve
- This is identity work to be with
- User needs witness, not fixer

### 6. Honor Identity Entanglement
- 18 years + 2 children = not simple separation
- Ongoing co-parenting = ongoing connection
- This is profound renegotiation, not clean break

## Conclusion

Semantic attunement means:
1. **Understanding what the words actually mean** (semantics, not syntax)
2. **Recognizing what the user is really asking** (implicit needs, not explicit questions)
3. **Responding to the deep work** (identity reconstruction, not situation management)
4. **Providing what's needed** (presence for uncertainty, not solutions for problems)

The difference between a pattern-matching chatbot and a semantically attuned system is this:

**Chatbot**: "Oh, you're dealing with divorce. Let me give you divorce recovery advice."

**Semantic System**: "You're renegotiating the fundamental question of who you are without 18 years of being undermined. The uncertainty isn't a problem - it's information that you're reconstructing. I'm here with you in that."
