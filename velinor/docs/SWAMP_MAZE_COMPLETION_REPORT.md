## 🧩 Three-Passage Structure

### Passage 1: The False Path
- **Gate**: Observation reveals moss patterns; Empathy senses forgotten presence
- **Outcome**: Success → forward; Failure → loop back
- **Lore**: "The swamp doesn't trust easy passages"

### Passage 2: The Fog Logic Puzzle
- **Gate**: Pure intuition (five emotion-shaped stones, no names given)
- **Solution**: The still/quiet stone (Stillness marker)
- **Mechanics**: Empathy, Observation, or pure trial-and-error can succeed
- **Lore**: "The swamp whispers. Just impressions."

### Passage 3: The Hidden Threshold
- **Gate**: Empathy unlocks "resonance encounter" (memory air)
- **Reward**: Tokens (Drossel's five emotional anchors)
- **Outcome**: Empathy +1, Observation +1, preparation for Drossel confrontation
- **Lore**: "You're holding his secrets now"
