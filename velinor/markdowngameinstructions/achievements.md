# Velinor Achievements

## Solo Path
- **Archivist**: Unlock 10 glyphs
- **Tonekeeper**: Unlock 35 glyphs
- **Remembrancer**: Unlock all 70 glyphs solo

## Co-Play Path
- **Twin Resonance (Final Achievement)**: Unlock all 70 glyphs with a friend  
  - Symbolizes that memory restoration is communal, not solitary.
  - Requires shared glyph discovery or co-play session.

## Hidden Achievements
- **Echo Listener**: Unlock lore fragments by listening to NPCs without interruption.
- **Collapse Witness**: Trigger all five Saori endings across multiple playthroughs.
- **Breathkeeper**: Speak 10 ceremonial phrases in Velinorian language during gameplay.
