## Core Glyphs

### Cinarä̈

- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ˈsɪˑnɑˑrə/ → ah-ha-ah
- **Literal Meaning**: Invoked beloved
- **Emotional Voltage**: Sacred invocation, soul-bonded presence
- **Diacritical Logic**: Triple diaeresis (̈̈) = cyclical breath, sanctified clarity
- **Breath Shape**: “ah-ha-ah” — triple exhalation, echoing invocation
- **Resonance Category**: Legacy / Trust
- **Gameplay Function**: Glows during vowcraft, memory restoration, shrine activation involving relational bonds
- **Lore Fragment**: “Cinarä̈ was whispered before the collapse, when love was lineage. To speak it now is to remember not just who, but why.”

##

### Brethielï̈

- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ˈbrɛθiːli/ → hsii
- **Literal Meaning**: Breath as guide
- **Emotional Voltage**: Guidance, patience, continuity
- **Diacritical Logic**: Double diaeresis (̈̈) = soft hiss, cyclical patience
- **Breath Shape**: “hsii” — soft hiss, tongue arched to palate
- **Resonance Category**: Presence / Ache
- **Gameplay Function**: Stabilizes collapsing structures, aids persuasion rolls, unlocks reconciliation endings
- **Lore Fragment**: “Brethielï̈ was taught to children as lullaby—breath as compass, breath as home.”

##

### Sha’rú

- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ʃaʔruː/ → kahʔ
- **Literal Meaning**: Repair, restore
- **Emotional Voltage**: Defiance, restoration, sacred duty
- **Diacritical Logic**: Glottal stop = wind caught in throat, vow of repair
- **Breath Shape**: Sharp halt, like wind trapped in stone
- **Resonance Category**: Sovereignty / Collapse
- **Gameplay Function**: Used to mend glyph fractures, stabilize resonance drift, unlock reconciliation or destruction endings depending on context
- **Lore Fragment**: “Sha’rú was vow and act alike—repairing tone was sacred duty.”

##

### Esḧ

- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ɛʃ/ → ah-ha
- **Literal Meaning**: You (sacred witness)
- **Emotional Voltage**: Witnessed clarity, sanctity of presence
- **Diacritical Logic**: Diaeresis (̈) = triple exhalation, cyclical breath
- **Breath Shape**: “ah-ha” — exhale of recognition
- **Resonance Category**: Presence / Legacy
- **Gameplay Function**: Invoked in dialogue to anchor NPC trust, glows during collective resonance events
- **Lore Fragment**: “Esḧ was the word of witness—spoken when one soul saw another without veil.”

##

### Vessent’

- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ˈvɛs.sɛnt/ → kah
- **Literal Meaning**: Vessel, bearer
- **Emotional Voltage**: Duty, containment, devotion
- **Diacritical Logic**: Apostrophe = whispered release, soft exhale
- **Breath Shape**: “kah” — whispered closure
- **Resonance Category**: Sovereignty / Trust
- **Gameplay Function**: Used in vowcraft to bind glyphs together, glows when player carries sacred memories
- **Lore Fragment**: “Vessent’ was spoken in shrines when one agreed to carry another’s ache.”
