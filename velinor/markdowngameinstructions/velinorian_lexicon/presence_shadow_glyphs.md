# Velinorian Glyphic Dictionary (Presence & Shadow Glyphs)

---

## Presence Glyphs

### Aelitḧ
- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ˈae.lɪθ/ → ah-ha  
- **Literal Meaning**: Stillness, witness  
- **Emotional Voltage**: Calm, clarity, sacred observation  
- **Diacritical Logic**: Diaeresis = sanctified clarity of presence  
- **Breath Shape**: “ah-ha” — steady exhale  
- **Resonance Category**: Presence  
- **Gameplay Function**: Glows when player listens without interruption, strengthens NPC trust  
- **Lore Fragment**: “Aelitḧ was spoken in shrines where silence itself was devotion.”

### Nivrä
- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ˈni.vra/ → ah-ha  
- **Literal Meaning**: Shelter, sanctuary  
- **Emotional Voltage**: Safety, refuge, continuity  
- **Diacritical Logic**: Diaeresis = cyclical breath of protection  
- **Breath Shape**: “ah-ha” — protective exhale  
- **Resonance Category**: Presence / Trust  
- **Gameplay Function**: Glows in safe zones, restores resonance points after collapse events  
- **Lore Fragment**: “Nivrä was the word of refuge, spoken when survivors gathered in hidden places.”

### Oshen̈
- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ˈo.ʃɛn/ → ineah  
- **Literal Meaning**: Flow, tide  
- **Emotional Voltage**: Continuity, patience, rhythm  
- **Diacritical Logic**: Tilde = nasal breath, flowing tone  
- **Breath Shape**: “ineah” — rolling exhale  
- **Resonance Category**: Presence / Ache  
- **Gameplay Function**: Unlocks dialogue tied to patience, glows in water-based environments  
- **Lore Fragment**: “Oshen̈ was sung by riverbanks, a breath that carried memory downstream.”

## Shadow Glyphs

### Mirrö
- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ˈmɪr.o/ → ah-ha  
- **Literal Meaning**: Reflection, doubt  
- **Emotional Voltage**: Suspicion, agency, self-questioning  
- **Diacritical Logic**: Diaeresis = cyclical breath of uncertainty  
- **Breath Shape**: “ah-ha” — wavering exhale  
- **Resonance Category**: Shadow / Doubt  
- **Gameplay Function**: Glows during interrogation branches, alters Saori’s responses  
- **Lore Fragment**: “Mirrö was the word of suspicion, spoken when trust faltered.”

### Querrä
- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ˈkwɛr.a/ → ah-ha  
- **Literal Meaning**: Question, inquiry  
- **Emotional Voltage**: Curiosity, suspicion  
- **Diacritical Logic**: Diaeresis = cyclical breath of inquiry  
- **Breath Shape**: “ah-ha” — probing exhale  
- **Resonance Category**: Doubt  
- **Gameplay Function**: Unlocks hidden dialogue options, glows when player presses NPCs further  
- **Lore Fragment**: “Querrä was the breath of curiosity, the wind that unsettled silence.”

### Shyrä
- **Phonetic Rendering**: /ˈʃaɪ.ra/ → ah-ha-ah  
- **Literal Meaning**: Concealment, secrecy  
- **Emotional Voltage**: Hidden truth, intimacy, suspicion  
- **Diacritical Logic**: Triple diaeresis = cyclical breath of concealment  
- **Breath Shape**: “ah-ha-ah” — layered exhale, fading tone  
- **Resonance Category**: Shadow / Trust  
- **Gameplay Function**: Glows when player withholds memories, unlocks Sacred Withholding ending  
- **Lore Fragment**: “Shyrä was whispered in secrecy, a vow to keep memory hidden.”

### Veyth̄
- **Phonetic Rendering**: /veɪθ/ → da-hah  
- **Literal Meaning**: Mask, performance  
- **Emotional Voltage**: Deception, illusion, agency  
- **Diacritical Logic**: Line accent = forceful breath, wind echo  
- **Breath Shape**: “da-hah” — sharp exhale of concealment  
- **Resonance Category**: Shadow / Collapse  
- **Gameplay Function**: Glows when player deceives NPCs, alters group resonance outcomes  
- **Lore Fragment**: “Veyth̄ was carved into masks worn by Saonyx’s performers, hiding ache behind spectacle.”
