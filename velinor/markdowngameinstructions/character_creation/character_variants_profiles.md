# Character Variants: Profiles & Mechanical Specs

## Overview

This document contains detailed profiles and mechanical specifications for each character variant in **Velinor: Remnants of the Tone**.

All three variants have access to the same story. The choice affects **tone**, **NPC perception**, and **mechanical subtleties**—not narrative gatekeeping.

---

## Variant 1: LIOR (Male)

### Visual Profile

**Appearance:**
- Height: Medium (170-175 cm)
- Build: Lean, not muscular—stamina-built rather than strength-built
- Face: Angular, sharp features; observant eyes; minimal softness
- Hair: Dark, short (shoulder-length or shorter), practical
- Skin: Weathered from rural living; sun-exposed
- Distinctive marks: Small scar on left hand (archival accident or childhood wound)

**Clothing (Default):**
- Archive tunic (gray-brown linen, loose fit)
- Under-layer: Travel-worn shirt (earth tones)
- Belt: Worn leather with utility pouch
- Boots: Practical, broken-in
- Cloak: Minimal; he prefers mobility

**Movement:**
- Deliberate, economical
- No wasted gesture; every movement has purpose
- Walks with slight forward lean (attentive, ready)
- When stressed: jaw tightens; hands still; presence becomes sharper

**Energy:**
- Quiet but not passive
- Alert; always observing surroundings
- Can be still for long periods without discomfort
- Presence is felt as competence, not charisma

---

### Character Baseline

**Stats:**
- Coherence: 40 (disoriented; recent trauma)
- Empathy: 55 (present and open, but guarded)
- Observation: 50 (attentive; notices patterns)
- Presence: 45 (uncertain of social role; not commanding)
- Memory: 35 (fragmented; recent loss blocks recall)

**Philosophy (Internal):**
- "Loss is permanent. Acceptance is survival."
- "I don't understand systems, but I respect precision."
- "Feeling too much is dangerous; attention keeps you alive."

**Fears:**
- Abandonment (parents' death still fresh)
- Institutional assimilation (becoming like the systems he doesn't understand)
- Irrelevance (not mattering to anyone)

---

### Dialogue Signature

**Tone:** Direct, reserved, occasionally vulnerable when pressed

**Speech Patterns:**
- Short sentences; minimal elaboration
- Uses "I" sparingly; prefers observation ("The work needs..." over "I want...")
- When emotional: words become even more clipped; rare vulnerability leaks through
- Questions are genuine, not rhetorical
- Compliments are rare and thus significant

**Example Dialogue (to Malrik):**
> "I understand. I'll do it correctly."
> [Later, if trust builds]
> "Thank you for letting me stay. I... I needed that."

**Example Dialogue (to Elenya):**
> "What is that ritual? Why do people gather for it?"
> [If attending]
> "I felt something there. Not explanation. Just... presence."

---

### Relationship Dynamics

**With Malrik:**
- Respects authority; competent work earns subtle approval
- Malrik sees Lior as reliable but distant
- Over time: Malrik may realize he's lonely and that Lior doesn't hate him
- Tension: Lior quietly doubts the Archive's rigid philosophy
- **Intimate moment:** Malrik admits (rarely) that he values Lior's presence, then returns to professionalism

**With Elenya:**
- Drawn to spiritual practice without understanding it
- Elenya senses his grief and doesn't probe; he appreciates this
- Over time: Elenya becomes someone he can be present with without explanation
- **Intimate moment:** Elenya's hand on his shoulder lingers, both recognize something unsaid, then gently release

**With Velinor:**
- Professional respect; Lior listens to teaching
- Velinor recognizes Lior's unintegrated state and sees potential
- Over time: Velinor becomes mentor figure; Lior struggles with vulnerability
- **Intimate moment:** Velinor asks directly: "Have you ever loved someone in that way?" Lior's answer becomes a bridge to deeper understanding

**With Ravi (Marketplace guide):**
- Easy camaraderie; Ravi doesn't pressure explanation
- Lior can be more himself with Ravi—relaxed, occasionally humorous
- Ravi treats Lior as peer rather than novice
- Connection: Based on shared outsider status

---

### Mechanical Notes

**Stat Progression:**
- Coherence rises when: Making choices independently, experiencing moments of clarity
- Empathy rises when: Witnessing others' pain, choosing compassion over efficiency
- Observation rises when: Studying NPCs, solving pattern-based puzzles
- Presence rises when: Standing up to authority, speaking truth despite fear
- Memory rises when: Unlocking glyphs, recovering parent memories

**Glyph Resonance (Gender-Specific):**
- **Sovereignty glyphs**: Respond strongly (male-coded autonomy resonance)
- **Trust glyphs**: Respond moderately (masculine trust patterns)
- **Joy glyphs**: Respond weakly (difficulty accessing unguarded joy)

**NPC Attunement:**
- Malrik: Starts at 0.05 (minimal); rises with demonstrated competence
- Elenya: Starts at 0.15 (she senses him immediately); rises with ritual attendance
- Velinor: Starts at 0.10 (professional); rises with philosophical conversations
- Ravi: Starts at 0.20 (warm); rises with casual interaction

---

### Character Arc Potential

**Act I (Arrival):** Lior arrives disoriented; finds work with Malrik; begins attending Elenya's rituals.

**Act II (Understanding):** Lior recognizes the love story embedded in glyphs; begins to question his own capacity for connection.

**Act III (Agency):** Lior must choose: support Malrik's institutional approach or Elenya's spiritual one? Or forge a third path?

**Act IV (Consciousness):** Final choice about Corelink system; Lior's personal autonomy vs. collective integration.

**Thematic Throughline:** From "loss means I accept anything" to "loss means I know what matters."

## Variant 2: LIOREN (Female)

### Visual Profile

**Appearance:**
- Height: Medium (170-175 cm)
- Build: Lean, lithe—grace inherent in posture
- Face: Angular, sharp features; expressive eyes; subtle asymmetry
- Hair: Dark, longer (shoulder-length or past shoulders), often loose or loosely braided
- Skin: Weathered from rural living; sun-exposed; freckles or marks from healing work
- Distinctive marks: Calluses on fingertips (from needlework, herb-gathering, or ritual); small burn mark on forearm (healing accident)

**Clothing (Default):**
- Archive tunic (gray-brown linen) worn with flowing overlay (ritual-influenced drape)
- Under-layer: Practical shirt (earth tones) + soft undershirt
- Belt: Woven cord with ritual tokens (small bells, stones)
- Boots: Practical, grounding
- Cloak: Draped rather than closed; fabric moves with her

**Movement:**
- Fluid, present, grounded
- Moves through space with awareness; not rushed
- When still: deeply present; not fidgeting
- When stressed: breathing deepens; hands move to heart; presence becomes more inward
- Hands are expressive; used to communicate even in silence

**Energy:**
- Warm but not effusive
- Present and emotionally available while maintaining boundaries
- Can hold others' emotion without being overwhelmed by it
- Presence is felt as recognition, not charisma

---

### Character Baseline

**Stats:**
- Coherence: 41 (slightly more grounded than Lior; emotional presence stabilizes)
- Empathy: 56 (deeply felt; able to be present with others' pain)
- Observation: 49 (intuitive more than analytical; reads people well)
- Presence: 46 (more commanding than Lior; people listen when she speaks)
- Memory: 35 (same fragmentation; loss blocks recall equally)

**Philosophy (Internal):**
- "Grief teaches us what matters. Honor that teaching."
- "Systems are made by people. People can be trusted or not."
- "Feeling deeply is what makes us human. That's worth the risk."

**Fears:**
- Being responsible for others' pain (healer's burden)
- Emotional isolation (being seen but not known)
- Institutional dismissal of intuitive knowledge

---

### Dialogue Signature

**Tone:** Thoughtful, reflective, with comfortable pauses; emotional but not unstable

**Speech Patterns:**
- Longer sentences; elaboration through metaphor
- Uses "I feel" and "I sense" alongside "I think"
- Comfortable with silence; doesn't rush conversation
- Questions often invite reflection rather than answer
- Compliments are specific and memorable
- Vulnerability is strength, not weakness

**Example Dialogue (to Malrik):**
> "I understand the precision you value. I also understand that some things can't be catalogued. Both are true."
> [Later, if trust builds]
> "You're so afraid of losing control. I see it. I'm afraid too. But differently."

**Example Dialogue (to Elenya):**
> "I feel drawn to your rituals. I don't understand them completely, but they feel true."
> [If deeper connection]
> "Can you teach me to hold grief without letting it consume me?"

---

### Relationship Dynamics

**With Malrik:**
- Respects his precision; challenges his rigidity gently
- Malrik notices her warmth and becomes slightly flustered; doesn't know what to do with that
- Over time: Malrik realizes that her emotional intelligence is another form of precision
- Tension: Lioren questions whether he's capable of feeling; Malrik denies it reflexively
- **Intimate moment:** Malrik nearly reaches for her hand during a difficult conversation, catches himself, maintains professionalism

**With Elenya:**
- Immediately recognized as spiritual peer
- Elenya sees Lioren as someone capable of deep glyph work
- Over time: Elenya teaches; Lioren learns; mutual respect deepens
- Connection: Spiritual resonance and shared intuitive knowledge
- **Intimate moment:** Elenya's hand touches Lioren's face to adjust a ritual marking; both feel the boundary moment; both honor it by continuing the ritual

**With Velinor:**
- Velinor respects her insights; sees her as capable of advanced study
- Lioren is curious about Velinor's knowledge; asks good questions
- Over time: Intellectual and emotional connection deepens
- **Intimate moment:** Velinor shares a personal memory; Lioren holds his vulnerability without judgment; they both understand something shifted but neither acts on it

**With Ravi (Marketplace guide):**
- Ravi is somewhat charmed; tries to flirt; Lioren gently maintains boundaries
- But genuine friendship emerges; Ravi respects her seriousness
- Connection: Ravi can make her laugh; she appreciates that

---

### Mechanical Notes

**Stat Progression:**
- Coherence rises when: Finding emotional clarity through reflection, accepting losses
- Empathy rises when: Supporting others, choosing compassion despite personal cost
- Observation rises when: Studying emotional patterns in NPCs, intuitive breakthroughs
- Presence rises when: Expressing truth authentically, holding space for others
- Memory rises when: Unlocking glyphs tied to healing, recovering mother's memory particularly

**Glyph Resonance (Gender-Specific):**
- **Legacy glyphs**: Respond strongly (female-coded ancestral resonance)
- **Presence glyphs**: Respond strongly (spiritual embodiment)
- **Collapse glyphs**: Respond with intense emotion (traumatic patterns)

**NPC Attunement:**
- Malrik: Starts at 0.08 (wary; she threatens his systems); rises slowly with patience
- Elenya: Starts at 0.25 (immediate recognition); rises quickly through ritual work
- Velinor: Starts at 0.15 (professional + respectful); rises with intellectual engagement
- Ravi: Starts at 0.22 (flirtation + genuine interest); rises through friendly interaction

---

### Character Arc Potential

**Act I (Arrival):** Lioren arrives grieving but present; finds spiritual community with Elenya; begins work with Malrik.

**Act II (Understanding):** Lioren recognizes the love story as a teaching about what institutions cannot hold; begins her own spiritual practice.

**Act III (Agency):** Lioren must choose: deepen spiritual practice or engage with systems? Or create integration?

**Act IV (Consciousness):** Final choice about Corelink system; Lioren's spiritual autonomy vs. collective healing.

**Thematic Throughline:** From "grief must be felt completely" to "grief transforms into wisdom."

## Variant 3: LIOR(EN) (Nonbinary)

### Visual Profile

**Appearance:**
- Height: Medium (170-175 cm)
- Build: Lean; neither distinctly masculine nor feminine
- Face: Neutral features; expressive eyes; ambiguous read
- Hair: Dark, shoulder-length, unstyled; neither long nor short enough to code strongly
- Skin: Weathered from rural living; sun-exposed
- Distinctive marks: Depends on player's specific coding; neutral by default

**Clothing (Default):**
- Archive tunic (gray-brown linen, unadorned, loose fit)
- Under-layer: Neutral shirt (earth tones)
- Belt: Simple leather, functional
- Boots: Practical, gender-neutral
- Cloak: Functional; no aesthetic flourish
- Overall: Deliberately androgynous; no gendered coding

**Movement:**
- Present and attentive; neither distinctly masculine nor feminine
- Fluid enough to suggest groundedness; direct enough to suggest focus
- When still: neither rigid nor soft; centered
- When stressed: presence becomes uncertain; questioning posture
- Hands are used functionally; not particularly expressive

**Energy:**
- Observant and present without claiming space
- Neither dominating nor deferring
- Available for connection but not seeking it
- Presence is felt as "becoming" rather than "arrived"

---

### Character Baseline

**Stats:**
- Coherence: 40.5 (average of both; slightly more grounded than Lior)
- Empathy: 55.5 (balanced between logic and emotion)
- Observation: 49.5 (intuitive and systematic; sees both patterns)
- Presence: 45.5 (uncertain of social role; could go either direction)
- Memory: 35 (equal fragmentation)

**Philosophy (Internal):**
- "I don't fit neatly. Maybe that's the point."
- "Both precision and feeling matter. I'm learning to hold both."
- "I'm not decided yet. I'm becoming."

**Fears:**
- Being forced to choose a fixed identity
- Irrelevance (not mattering because I don't fit categories)
- Never understanding themselves

---

### Dialogue Signature (Integrated Name: Lior(en))

**Tone:** Flexible, shifting between direct and reflective; comfortable with uncertainty

**Speech Patterns:**
- Code-switches between styles depending on context and conversation partner
- Uses "I think/feel/know" interchangeably
- Comfortable with "I'm not sure" as a valid stance
- Questions come from genuine curiosity, not challenge
- Vulnerability is present but careful; trust-dependent
- Can be playful or serious; adapts to interlocutor

**Example Dialogue (to Malrik):**
> "I understand the system, but I also sense there's something it can't hold. Am I wrong?"
> [Later, if trust builds]
> "You remind me of someone. Maybe I remind myself."

**Example Dialogue (to Elenya):**
> "I feel something in the rituals, but I don't have the language for it. Can you help?"
> [If deeper connection]
> "I think I understand what you're trying to preserve. It's not about holding it. It's about being held by it."

### Secondary Name Variants

#### If Player Chooses "Lior" (Within Lior(en) Variant)

**Modified Profile:**
- Visual: Androgynous with masculine lean (shorter hair, minimal adornment)
- Dialogue tone: Skews toward Lior's directness, with occasional Lioren softness
- NPC perception: "Androgynous person going by Lior"
- Stat baseline: Exact Lior baseline
- Pronoun signature: "he/him"

**Unique Element:** NPCs may notice the name/presentation mismatch and react differently:
- Malrik: Uses "Lior" but doesn't specifically gender the person
- Elenya: May refer to "one who moves between forms"
- Ravi: Uses "Lior" and is genuinely inclusive; doesn't make it weird

---

#### If Player Chooses "Lioren" (Within Lior(en) Variant)

**Modified Profile:**
- Visual: Androgynous with feminine lean (longer hair, subtle adornment)
- Dialogue tone: Skews toward Lioren's reflection, with occasional Lior directness
- NPC perception: "Androgynous person going by Lioren"
- Stat baseline: Exact Lioren baseline
- Pronoun signature: "she/her"

**Unique Element:** NPCs may notice the name/presentation mismatch and react differently:
- Malrik: Uses "Lioren" and is professionally cordial; doesn't acknowledge the androgyny
- Elenya: May refer to "one who refuses to be contained"
- Ravi: Uses "Lioren" and is warm; the gender-neutral presentation doesn't change interaction

---

#### If Player Chooses "Lior(en)" (Fully Integrated)

**Profile (as above):**
- Visual: Purely androgynous (shoulder-length hair, no gendered adornment)
- Dialogue tone: Flexibly code-switches based on context
- NPC perception: "Person who exists between categories"
- Stat baseline: Average of both variants (as above)
- Pronoun signature: "they/them"

**Unique Element:** NPCs respond to the integrated identity:
- Malrik: Says "Lior(en)" or occasionally just "Lior"; respects the choice without fully understanding
- Elenya: May use "seeker" or "Lior(en)"; recognizes the chosen integration
- Velinor: Uses "Lior(en)" formally; appreciates the clarity of the choice
- Ravi: Uses "Lior(en)" casually; no big deal; just another person

---

### Relationship Dynamics

**With Malrik:**
- Lior(en) represents everything his institutional logic can't categorize
- Malrik is both frustrated and intrigued by the refusal to be fixed
- Over time: Malrik may come to see that some things resist categorization
- Tension: Malrik questions what Lior(en) "really" is; Lior(en) refuses the question
- **Intimate moment:** Malrik asks directly: "Are you lonely? Is that why you keep coming to the rituals?" The question itself is the intimacy; Lior(en)'s answer creates understanding

**With Elenya:**
- Elenya immediately recognizes Lior(en) as someone "becoming"
- Lior(en) feels safe in Elenya's space because Elenya doesn't demand resolution
- Over time: Elenya becomes spiritual guide; Lior(en) becomes her mirror
- Connection: Shared understanding that some truths don't resolve; they integrate
- **Intimate moment:** Elenya and Lior(en) reach for each other in ritual; the touch is profound; both understand the depth and release it gently

**With Velinor:**
- Velinor appreciates Lior(en)'s refusal to fit; sees it as philosophical sophistication
- Lior(en) is drawn to Velinor's teaching precisely because it holds paradox
- Over time: Intellectual connection deepens; emotional vulnerability follows
- **Intimate moment:** Velinor admits uncertainty about what he feels; Lior(en) honors the honesty; they continue the conversation unchanged

**With Ravi (Marketplace guide):**
- Ravi treats Lior(en) as just another person; doesn't make the gender-neutrality weird
- Lior(en) appreciates this normalization
- Connection: Easy, warm, uncomplicated friendship
