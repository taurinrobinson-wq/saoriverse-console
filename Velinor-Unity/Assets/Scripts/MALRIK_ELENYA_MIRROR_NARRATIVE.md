## 🔄 The Mirror Structure

### BEFORE COLLAPSE: Shared Truth

Both lived one love story—silent, grounded, without words.

Malrik: Precise mind learning that presence > structure  
Elenya: Spiritual heart learning that love ≠ possession

```
           LIGHTHOUSE
        (shared sanctuary)
            /      \
      Malrik        Elenya
     Structure    Presence
   (reconciling) (reconciling)
```

### THE COLLAPSE: Same Moment, Different Wounds

The cataclysm severs their shared memory.

Malrik wakes: "I lost something precious and I can't remember what"  
Elenya wakes: "I feel an ache I can't name and it's killing me"

### POST-COLLAPSE PATHS: Opposite Responses to Same Loss

**MALRIK**: Unconsciously tries to remember
- Unconsciously restored archive (trying to reconstruct order)
- Unconsciously drawn to lighthouse (trying to recreate sacred space)
- Unconsciously cannot escape the ache (body memory)
- Body remembers what mind cannot

**ELENYA**: Consciously chooses to forget  
- Performs ritual of forgetting (choosing to release)
- Becomes High Seer (teaching what she cannot live)
- Teaches from the wound (authentic breaking)
- Spirit remembers what she tried to sever

### THE TURNING POINT: Objects as Keys

**MALRIK**: Finds broken lantern → Knows he must fix it → Restores lighthouse  
**ELENYA**: Finds broken lantern → Feels something crack → Visits lighthouse

Same object. Same effect. Different entry points.

### THE REUNION: The Moment Everything Changes

```
          RESTORED LIGHTHOUSE
               (light)
          /             \
      Malrik         Elenya
   "I don't know   "I chose to
    why I fixed     forget you"
    this"           
      \            /
       RECOGNITION
       (not memory,
        but knowing)
```

**Malrik**: "I remember... something. Someone. A presence. And I remember losing it."

**Elenya**: "I remember loving someone. And I remember choosing to forget because the half-memory was killing me."

He grieves what he cannot name.  
She confesses what she deliberately erased.

And then: They see each other.

## 🔮 Design Principles

### 1. MUTUAL VULNERABILITY
Both arcs require player to recognize and honor their broken places.
- Malrik: Grieves what he can't articulate
- Elenya: Made a mercy choice that cost her

### 2. PARALLEL JOURNEYS, SEPARATE ARCS
Neither is dependent on the other until the reunion.
- Player can complete Malrik without Elenya (and vice versa)
- But *knowing both* completes the truth of each

### 3. GATE-BASED PROGRESSION
- Player choice determines which stat paths unlock which dialogue
- High empathy path: Unlock vulnerable admissions
- High observation path: Unlock subtle truth-telling
- High coherence path: Unlock paradox acceptance

### 4. OBJECT AS METAPHOR
The broken lighthouse lantern is the same object for both:
- For Malrik: A compulsion to fix → restoring
- For Elenya: A crack in forgetting → remembering

### 5. RECOGNITION WITHOUT RESTORATION
Final truth: They don't need full memory restored. They need each other present.
